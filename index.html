Academic and professional history

I completed my higher secondary education in Thuraiyur and obtained my Law Degree from Salem Central Law College in 1997. After graduating, I started my legal career as a junior advocate under M. Sanjaiyan in Coimbatore, where I worked from 1997 to 2000.
In 2000, I returned to Thuraiyur and continued practicing under Advocate Ganeshen until 2003. These years gave me strong practical experience, especially in civil and settlement-related cases.
In 2003, I began my independent practice, opening my own office and taking on clients directly..
